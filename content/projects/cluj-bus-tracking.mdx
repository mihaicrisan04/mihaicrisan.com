A live tracker for Cluj-Napoca's public buses, built in Swift/SwiftUI while I was commuting to UBB every day.

## Why

The official Cluj transit app is sluggish — its ETA is often stale by a couple of minutes, which is enough to miss the bus. I wanted something I'd actually pull out of my pocket at the stop and trust the number on screen.

## What it does

- live bus positions on the map, updating as you watch
- per-stop ETA for the route you tapped, not just the nearest vehicle
- minimal UI — open, look, close

## How

Data comes from the public [TranzyAPI](https://tranzy.dev/) GTFS-realtime feed. The interesting part was the refresh strategy: instead of polling on a fixed timer, the app batches calls and skips re-renders where the upstream timestamps haven't moved. Perceived refresh stays near-instant even on patchy mobile networks, without burning battery.
