a Raycast extension for sending clipboard content (links, text, images, files) to WhatsApp — to yourself or any contact — without ever leaving the keyboard.

## why

WhatsApp is the de-facto inbox for everyone i talk to, but switching to it just to forward a link to myself is friction. this turns the "send to self" trick into a Raycast command: copy something, hit a shortcut, done.

## how it works

a small local daemon (built on [Baileys](https://github.com/WhiskeySockets/Baileys)) holds a single linked-device session to WhatsApp Web. Raycast commands talk to the daemon over IPC, so individual sends are instant and never spawn a browser.

the auth flow is a one-time QR scan from Raycast itself — the daemon then persists credentials and auto-restarts on login.

## commands

- **send to yourself** — clipboard → your number, no UI
- **send to contact** — search any contact, send
- **pick item to send** — choose from the last 6 clipboard entries
- **whatsapp auth** — show the QR inside Raycast
- **whatsapp daemon status** — health check
