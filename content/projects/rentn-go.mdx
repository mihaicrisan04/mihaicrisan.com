a car rental platform built for a local business in cluj-napoca — the goal was a complete digital storefront plus admin tools, replacing the manual phone-and-WhatsApp booking process they were running before.

## the frontend

a clean, fast catalog with filtering, real-time availability, and a streamlined checkout. pricing is dynamic: longer rentals automatically get a per-day discount, and configurable seasonal multipliers handle peak periods.

## the admin dashboard

the owner sees the full picture in one place — active rentals, upcoming reservations, fleet status, and a calendar view per vehicle. new bookings land in realtime thanks to Convex's reactive queries, so they don't have to refresh.

## services

- **standard rentals** — daily and weekly with flexible pickup
- **airport transfers** — dedicated flow with Mapbox-based routing and meet-and-greet
- **long-term leasing** — monthly packages at discounted rates
- **chauffeur services** — driver-included bookings for events and business trips

## stack notes

Convex handles the database, backend functions, and realtime sync — no separate API layer. Clerk does auth for both customers and the admin role. the whole thing is i18n'd from day one (RO/EN) because the customer base is split.
